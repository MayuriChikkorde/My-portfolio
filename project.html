Academic Project

DESIGN AND FABRICATION OF STEP SOLAR OVEN USING SENSIBLE HEAT AS STORAGE MEDIUM FOR REFUGEE CAMPS

Project based on Solar Oven
To design and fabricate step solar oven for refugee camp.
In today's world due to war many communities are becoming refugees, these refugee community are struggling to cook food when they are isolated from their country. Keeping in mind Step Solar Oven is designed and developed and provided to refuges or village peoples. A small change in the design is also made to increase the efficiency of Solar oven. That is making a step in solar oven instead of box. The steps nearer to glass they will receive maximum heat as compared to bottom placed cooking pots.
